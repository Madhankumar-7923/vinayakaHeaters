Manifold Heater

Manifold heaters are supplied in Chrome Nickel Steel with nominal sheath diameters of 6.80 mm, 8.00 mm, and 10.70 mm, and standard elements are designed with a threaded terminal post providing a minimum cold section of approximately 40 mm. The square section tubular design ensures excellent heat exchange between the heater surface and the manifold, and installation is carried out by carefully hammering the heaters into the square-section groove over the manifold and pressing them in place, which provides strong surface contact.

Specifications

Features

  • Durable Material – Constructed with high-quality Chrome Nickel Steel for strength and reliability.
  • Multiple Sizes – Available in sheath diameters of 6.80 mm, 8.00 mm, and 10.70 mm.
  • Threaded Terminal Post – Provides a minimum cold section of approximately 40 mm for safe operation.
  • Efficient Heat Transfer – Square section tubular design ensures excellent thermal exchange with the manifold.
  • Easy Installation – Designed to be pressed into square-section grooves for secure fitting and long service life.

Applications

  • Processing machinery.
  • Injection and Blow Moulding.
  • Hot runner bushings.
  • Packaging machinery.
  • For fastening of turbine bolts .
  • Hot plates, moulds and dies
